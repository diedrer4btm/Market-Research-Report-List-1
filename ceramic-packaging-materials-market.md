<p><p>The ceramic packaging materials market consists of three primary types: mechanical, thermal, and electrical ceramic materials. Mechanical ceramic materials are designed for strength and durability, ideal for structural applications. Thermal ceramic materials possess excellent heat resistance and insulation properties, suitable for high-temperature environments. Electrical ceramic materials exhibit high dielectric strength and conductivity, making them essential for electronic components and devices. This diverse range of ceramic materials enhances performance and reliability across various industries, including aerospace, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
