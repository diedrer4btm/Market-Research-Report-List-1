<p><strong>The Intra-Uterine Contraceptive Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Copper IUCD</li><li>Hormonal IUCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intra-uterine contraceptive device (IUCD) market comprises two main types: Copper IUCDs and Hormonal IUCDs. Copper IUCDs emit copper ions that create a toxic environment for sperm, preventing fertilization, and can remain effective for up to 10 years. Hormonal IUCDs release hormones, such as progestin, which thicken cervical mucus and thin the uterine lining to inhibit implantation. Both types are highly effective, long-term solutions for birth control, catering to different user preferences and medical needs.</p></p>
