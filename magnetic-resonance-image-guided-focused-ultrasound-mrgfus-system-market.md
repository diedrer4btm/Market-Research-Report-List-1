<p><p>The Magnetic Resonance Image-Guided Focused Ultrasound (MRgFUS) system market is categorized into three main types: extracorporeal devices, intracavitary devices, and interstitial devices. Extracorporeal devices operate from outside the body, using focused ultrasound waves to target tissues non-invasively. Intracavitary devices are inserted into body cavities, allowing for targeted treatment within specific areas, often enhancing precision. Interstitial devices are implanted directly into the tissue, providing localized treatment, ideal for conditions such as tumors or therapeutic interventions requiring exact targeting.</p></p>
