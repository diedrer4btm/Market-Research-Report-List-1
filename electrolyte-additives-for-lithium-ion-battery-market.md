<p><p>Electrolyte additives enhance the performance and stability of lithium-ion batteries. Vinylene carbonate (VC) forms a robust solid electrolyte interphase, improving cycle life. Fluorinated ethylene carbonate (FEC) enhances the thermal stability and overall safety of batteries. 1,3-Propane sultone (1,3-PS) improves ionic conductivity. Vinyl ethylene carbonate (VEC) aids in lithium-ion mobility and reduces electrolyte decomposition. Lithium bis(fluorosulfonyl)imide (LiFSI) contributes to high ionic conductivity and stability, while other additives may offer unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
