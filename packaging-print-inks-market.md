<p><p>The packaging print inks market encompasses various printing technologies, each with unique applications. Lithographic printing uses flat plates for high-quality images, ideal for large runs and detailed graphics. Gravure printing employs engraved cylinders for continuous printing, best suited for high-volume packaging. Flexographic printing uses flexible relief plates, making it versatile for various substrates, especially in food packaging. Digital printing, on the other hand, allows for quick, on-demand printing with minimal setup, catering to short runs and customization needs.</p></p>
