<p><p>The polyester films market is divided into two main types: rigid and flexible polyester films. Rigid polyester films are characterized by their sturdy structure, making them ideal for applications requiring durability and protection, such as packaging, electrical insulation, and industrial uses. Conversely, flexible polyester films are thinner and more pliable, suited for applications like food packaging and label production. Both types offer excellent barrier properties, chemical resistance, and versatility, contributing significantly to their growing demand across various industries.</p></p>
