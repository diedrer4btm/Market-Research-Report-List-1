<p><p>The desktop spectrophotometer market includes various types tailored for specific analytical needs. Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometers measure metal concentrations by analyzing light absorption. Fluorescence Spectrophotometers detect emitted light from samples, useful in biochemistry. UV-Visible Spectrophotometers measure light absorption in the UV and visible spectrum, widely used in chemical analysis. Infrared Spectrophotometers assess molecular vibrations, valuable in material characterization. Visible Spectrophotometers focus on light in the visible range, often used in educational and laboratory settings for colorimetric analysis.</p></p>
