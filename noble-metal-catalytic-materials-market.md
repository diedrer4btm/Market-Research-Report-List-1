<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Catalysis</li><li>Heterogeneous Catalysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The noble metal catalytic materials market encompasses two primary types: homogeneous and heterogeneous catalysis. Homogeneous catalysis involves catalysts that are in the same phase as the reactants, typically liquid solutions, resulting in uniform reactions and easier product separation. In contrast, heterogeneous catalysis involves solid catalysts that operate at different phases from reactants, often enhancing reaction rates and facilitating easier product recovery. Both types leverage noble metals for their superior activity and selectivity in various industrial and chemical processes.</p></p>
